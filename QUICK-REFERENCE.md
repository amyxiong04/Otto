# 🦦 OtterMatch - Quick Reference Card

## Project Overview
**Name:** OtterMatch  
**Tagline:** "Find a job that matches your vibe, not just your resume"  
**Mascot:** Otto the Otter 🦦  
**Aesthetic:** Whimsical, Ghibli-inspired, warm and friendly

---

## Core Concept
Traditional job platforms = Formal, transactional, stressful  
OtterMatch = Casual chat with a friend who helps you find work you'll love

---

## Key Differentiators

| Feature | Traditional Platforms | OtterMatch |
|---------|----------------------|------------|
| **Interface** | Forms and filters | Phone call + chat |
| **Tone** | Professional, formal | Casual, friendly |
| **Process** | Upload resume → Apply | Chat naturally → Discover |
| **Focus** | Skills matching | Values + personality fit |
| **Feel** | Job interview | Coffee with a friend |

## Otto's Personality Traits

✅ **Friendly:** Uses emojis, casual language  
✅ **Curious:** Asks open-ended questions  
✅ **Patient:** No pressure to answer quickly  
✅ **Empathetic:** Validates feelings  
✅ **Playful:** Adds fun facts and jokes  
✅ **Helpful:** Genuinely wants you to succeed  

---

## Conversation Topics (In Order)

1. **Ice Breaker:** "How's your day going?"
2. **Work Energy:** What energizes vs. drains you?
3. **Work Style:** Solo or team? Structured or flexible?
4. **Values:** What matters most? (impact, growth, balance, etc.)
5. **Deal Breakers:** What's non-negotiable?
6. **Past Experiences:** What have you loved/hated in previous roles?
7. **Future Dreams:** Where do you see yourself?

---

## User Journey

```
1. Incoming Call Screen
   ↓ (Accept)
2. Greeting & Ice Breaker
   ↓
3. Casual Conversation (5-10 messages)
   ↓
4. Resume Upload (Optional)
   ↓
5. Deeper Questions
   ↓
6. Job Matches Revealed
   ↓
7. Apply or Keep Chatting
```

## Common Questions

**Q: Why an otter?**  
A: Otters are social, playful, and hold hands - represents friendship and support in the job search journey.

**Q: Will this work on mobile?**  
A: Desktop first for prototype, mobile responsive in Phase 2.

**Q: How is this different from a chatbot?**  
A: It's not just Q&A - it's a guided conversation with personality and emotional intelligence.

**Q: What if users don't want to chat?**  
A: They can decline the call or upload a resume directly for traditional matching.
